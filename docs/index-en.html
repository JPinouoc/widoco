Budge hotel ratingback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If Hotel has rating lower than 3, then it is a budget hotel

lessThan(?) , Hotel c(?h) , ratingHotel dp(?h,?r) -> BudgetHotel c(?h)

Fast Food restaurant ratingback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If rating is less than 2, then it is a fast food restaurant

rating dp(?r,?rat) , Restaurant c(?r) , lessThan(?) -> FastFoodRestaurant c(?r)

Line3 Metroback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Line3 is metro

name dp(?t,"Line3") , Transport c(?t) -> Metro c(?t)
